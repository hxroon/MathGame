Meeting Notes, Estimate Quality Template and SOP Workstream

Purpose

The meeting focused on building a practical template to assess the quality of P&L estimates across Product Control. This supports the broader goals of moving the P&L close timeline earlier and improving actual-to-estimate commentary automation.

Main objective

The goal is to have the template ready by July 15. The first version does not need to capture every detail. It should capture enough information, around 80%, so the team can begin analysis and identify key themes.

Estimate quality template

The template should help determine:

What level estimates are received at

Whether estimates are detailed or rolled up

How estimates compare to actuals

What causes differences between estimates and actuals

What process impact the differences create

What actions are needed to improve estimate quality

Comments

Estimate versus actuals

Actuals are available at a more detailed P&L driver level, such as new trading, portfolio revaluation, originations, carry, theta, and other drivers.

Estimates may not always be available at the same level. Some teams may only provide one rolled-up number. The template should make this gap visible by allowing both estimate and actuals to be captured by similar P&L drivers where possible.

Consistency

Some fields may be calculated or pre-populated using existing data instead of asking analysts to manually complete everything.

Root cause and impact

The template should include free-text sections for root cause, process impact, and action required.

This will help capture whether issues lead to restatements, recalculations, manual work, delayed close timing, commentary changes, or follow-up with Front Office or system teams.

Materiality threshold

A threshold such as $50K was discussed, but it may need to remain flexible. The team should consider both rolled-up variances and offsetting movements that may net to zero but still contain material differences.

Data collection approach

Not every team will complete the template at the same pace. That is okay. The team can begin analyzing responses as they come in and use a smaller sample size to identify initial themes.

SOP SharePoint

SharePoint will be used only for final converted AI-generated SOPs. Existing source documents will remain in each team’s current folders. The SharePoint location will help store and track finalized converted SOPs.

Talent mobility deck point
